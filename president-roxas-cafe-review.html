A Cozy Café Find in President Roxas: Honest Review

June 27, 2026

There are cafés that you visit just for the food, and there are cafés that make you want to stay a little longer because of the atmosphere. This café in President Roxas gave me both comfort and a pleasant dining experience. From its calming interiors to its thoughtful service, my visit felt simple, relaxed, and worth sharing.

Location and Ambiance

The café is located in President Roxas, beside L&N Agriculture Supply. It is easy to find since it is situated along the highway, making it accessible for both locals and travelers passing through the area.

One of the first things I noticed was the café’s interior. The cream and white color palette gives the place a clean, soft, and elegant look. It has an aesthetic appeal without feeling too overwhelming. The ambiance is cozy and comfortable, making it a good place for casual dining, catching up with friends, or simply enjoying a quiet café moment.

Menu and Service

What makes the café even more interesting is the variety of food and drinks on their menu. They offer a wide selection, including appetizers, rice meals, pasta, burgers, pizza, and native dishes such as chicken and seafood options. For drinks, they serve milk tea, soda, frappes, and coffee, giving customers plenty of choices depending on their cravings.

Aside from the menu, the service was one of the highlights of my visit. The staff were thoughtful and accommodating. As soon as I arrived, they served water with ice, which was a small gesture but made a good impression. After a few moments, they brought the menu to my table and took my order.

This setup felt different from many cafés here in Capiz, where customers usually pay as they order. In this café, you are welcomed, seated, served with water, given time to browse the menu, and assisted with your order. Payment is done after eating, which gives the experience a more relaxed and restaurant-like feel.

Food and Drink Review

Although I was not able to try everything on the menu, I did enjoy their chicken pesto pasta. It was flavorful, satisfying, and definitely something I would order again. The serving and taste made it a good choice for anyone who enjoys creamy and savory pasta dishes.

For the drinks, I tried their milk tea. It was good, but personally, I found it a bit too sweet for my preference. However, for those who enjoy sweeter milk tea, it may still be a satisfying option.

Final Thoughts

Overall, my experience at this café was enjoyable. I loved the ambiance, the wide menu selection, and most especially the thoughtful service. The place feels welcoming, comfortable, and perfect for spending time with friends or family.

It is a café I would gladly visit again, and next time, I would love to come back with friends to try more dishes from their menu.
